African Child's Day is celebrated on the 16th of June every
year.Here in Educare, we celebrate it on a day mapped
out in the third term of every academic session to celebrate
our African culture. On this day, pupils and staff are to come
to school dressed in their own traditional attires along with
delicacies from their different tribes and cultures. Different
cultural dances and activies are performed. It is usually a
Funfilled Day

Art Club

Here in Art Club, pupils are helped to develop and enhance their Artistic skills and talent. They are further encouraged to create things with these skills.

Young Farmer's Club

The essence of the Young Farmer's Club is to develop and build up an appreciation for agriculture and also build up Agricultural skills in pupils.

Reading Club

The purpose of the Reading Club is to teach the children (younger ones- Junior Reading Club ) to read. To grow & nurture the reading culture in them (both Junior & Senior Reading Clubs).
